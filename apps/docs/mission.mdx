# Our Mission

As education inevitably becomes increasingly commodified, socioeconomic disparities yield outsized impacts over student outcomes.  
The OpenCourseWare Project (OCW) is dedicated to providing free, high-quality resources to students at Cherry Creek High School to help bridge these gaps in education.
